## 🏞️ The Vision

**Vietnam is one of the last untouched wellness destinations on Earth.**

- 3,260km of pristine coastline with hidden beach retreats

- 4,000 years of Eastern medicine, herbal wisdom, and healing tradition
- 54 ethnic cultures, each with unique restorative practices

Yet most travelers never find the *real* Vietnam — they end up in tourist traps, fake-reviewed hotels, and generic resorts that could be anywhere in the world.

**HealVN changes that.** We surface the *authentic havens* — the small eco-lodge in Hà Giang's buckwheat flower valleys, the grandmother herbalist in Hội An, the sunrise yoga spot that only locals know. We make these real, verifiable, and bookable in 60 seconds.

## ✅ 7-Layer Trust Verification System

Every retreat on HealVN passes through our verification stack:

| Layer | Check | Who Does It |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Identity** | Owner ID + business registration | HealVN Ops |
| **2. Legal** | Business license, tax, fire safety | HealVN Legal |
| **3. Physical** | On-site inspection by HealVN team | Field Team |
| **4. Visual** | Photo/video audit — no filters, no CGI | Content Team |
| **5. Reviews** | Only from confirmed, paid bookings | Platform |
| **6. Pricing** | Price transparency, no hidden fees | Algorithm |
| **7. Ongoing** | Monthly re-check, real-time rating monitoring | AI System |
